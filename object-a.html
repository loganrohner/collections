A SIMPLE SKULL

A raccoon skull. It is at least 38 years old. Originally found in Haliburton, Ontario.

Around 1983, in the forest near my cottage my mom and grandfather would walk through the area and sometimes find bones of animals.

They would find all sorts of skulls and bones ranging from a whole snapping turtle to a hawk skull.

This raccoon skull now ‘lives’ in a shadow box in my family’s house along side other skulls and arrow heads, preserved in a shadow box. Displaying their untold story.
